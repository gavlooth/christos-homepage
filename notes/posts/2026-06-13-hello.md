title: A small place to think out loud
date: 2026-06-13
summary: Why these notes exist and what I expect to write here.
---

I have wanted a quiet corner to write for a while — not a platform, not a feed,
just a place where ideas can sit and be revised.

This is that place. I expect it to stay close to the things I keep returning to:

- software and systems, especially in Clojure
- papers worth a second reading
- the seam between mathematics and engineering
- whatever I happen to be rethinking at the moment

These notes will lean short and unfinished on purpose. If something turns out to be
wrong, I would rather correct it in the open than pretend it was never written.

> The point is not to be right the first time. It is to keep a record honest enough
> that being wrong is cheap.

More soon.
